Separated bike lanes are exactly what they sound like, bike lanes with separation from a road, this could be through flex posts, curbs, or even on-street parking. Bike boulevards are roads designed to share car and bike traffic. The issues plaguing the Reno bike network are a lack of safety, network connectivity, and political will.
